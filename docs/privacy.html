Privacy Policy

Personal data

OptiSearch does not collect searching history, browser history, or any other personal data.

In future OptiSearch may collect browser version, platform name, display settings and user's OptiSearch settings (but never data which can help identify user). Such information could be needed for decision on implementing or removing features as well as finding bugs.

It will happen only with your permission.

Local Storage

OptiSearch uses Chrome (Chromium) or WebExtensions Storage Sync API for storing user's settings, as well as files (written by the developpers of the extension) fetched from Github Gist necessary for the extension to work.

Third party services

Donations are processed by the payment processor PayPal whose privacy policy is available here.
